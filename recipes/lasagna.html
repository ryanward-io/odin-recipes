Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish made by layering the (lasagna) sheets, sauce (vegetarian for our purposes) and cheese.

The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving rectangular portions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Vegetables
  • Tomato passata
  • Cheese

Recipe

  1. Get your pasta
  2. Make your sauce by mixing roasted vegetables and tomato passata
  3. Layer and put in oven
  4. Eat!
