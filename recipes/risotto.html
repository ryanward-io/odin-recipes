Risotto

Description

Risotto is a rice dish made by letting the rice soak up a vegetable broth

The resulting meal has a creamy texture and is served with shaved parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Vegetables (pumpkin, mushroom, spinach, onion)
  • Parmesan cheese

Recipe

  1. Cook the onion
  2. Add in rice and vegetable broth
  3. Once rice is al dente, it is ready
  4. Eat with a generous sprinkle of parmesan
